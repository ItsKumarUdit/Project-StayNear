StayNear

StayNear is a website that helps students find hostels and paying guest (PG) accommodations near their colleges or schools. Many students face difficulties when they move to a new city for studies, especially in finding a safe and comfortable place to stay. StayNear solves this problem by showing listings of hostels and PGs along with important details like location, facilities, rent, and contact information. Students can search based on their preferred area, compare options, and choose the one that suits them best. It makes the process faster, easier, and less stressful for students and their families.

Why choose us?

  • ✅ Fast Search: Instantly find hostels and PGs based on your location and preferences.
  • ✅ Verified Listings: All properties are verified to ensure safety and trust.
  • ✅ Student-Friendly: Affordable, comfortable, and tailored for student needs.
  • ✅ Easy Comparison: View rent, facilities, and contact details side-by-side.
  • ✅ Budget Filters: Quickly filter hostels by your preferred rent range.
  • ✅ Nearby Landmarks: See distance from colleges, markets, and bus stops.
  • ✅ Real Photos: Browse actual hostel images before visiting.
  • ✅ Direct Contact: Call or message owners right from the platform.
  • ✅ Bookmark Listings: Save your favorite hostels for later.
